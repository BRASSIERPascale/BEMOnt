Introduction back to ToC

The HIT2GAP data model propose an energetic contextualization of a building within a micro grid through the integration of the OntoMG ontology [1]. In this kind of modelling, the grid (micro-grid) is based on several branches that link the building infrastructure with the generated building renewable flows, non-renewable flows and the different building systems linked with that flows. It was aligned to IFC through several concepts: - Onto_MG:BranchController, isA, ifc:Controller - Onto_MG:InfraBranch, isA, ifc:DistributionCircuit - Onto_MG:DERBranch, isA, ifc:DistributionCircuit - Onto_MG:ESBranch, isA, ifc:DistributionElement - Onto_MG:ELBranch “isA” ifc:DistributionElement.
