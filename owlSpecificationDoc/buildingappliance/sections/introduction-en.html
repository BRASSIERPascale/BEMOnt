Building appliance corresponds with the real world representation of the building elements. This elements could be static or mobile elements. In fact, this building elements contains building systems ("Flow Instruments", "ProtectiveDevices", "Unitary Control Elements"). But also, this specific abstracted views could contain sensors and building appliance for monitoring and control of the building, incorporate occupants devices and the incorporation of building specific sensors.
